This was one of my first assignments in the real world :). Chiro Hekeko always does a little contest for the graphic deisgn of their festival, so I submitted my design and won. Hekeko stands for Heverlee, Kessel-lo and Korbeeklo, so I used the geographical borders on the poster and gave them a 3D effect to let it pop out more. After coming up with this concept I just started playing with the lay-out and came up with this!
